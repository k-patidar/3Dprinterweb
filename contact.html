Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you accept?

We accept STL, OBJ, 3MF, and most CAD file formats. If you have a different format, contact us and we'll help convert it.

How quickly can you complete my project?

Turnaround times vary by project complexity. Simple prototypes can be ready same-day, while complex projects typically take 3-7 business days.

Do you offer design services?

Yes! Our expert design team can create 3D models from sketches, photos, or your ideas. We also offer design optimization for better printability.

What's your minimum order quantity?

We have no minimum order quantity. Whether you need one prototype or thousands of parts, we can accommodate your needs.
